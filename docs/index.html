Welcome to Aurora!

What is Aurora?

Aurora is a private discord bot for the discord server Aurora!
It helps us at Aurora manage our server and customise it in more advanced ways than would normally be possible.

Can I get Aurora myself?

Due to the bot being a private bot for our server, it is specialised for our server and wont work on other servers.
However, the bot is partly open source, so you can take a look at the source code and take inspiration off of it for your
own discord bot.
